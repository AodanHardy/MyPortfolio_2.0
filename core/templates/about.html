About Me

Background

I began my journey in programming a couple of years before I started studying computing at NRC. While I was still studying business, I started following a few Python beginner tutorials on YouTube and also started learning how to use some more advanced Python libraries such as pygame where I created a few small games just as a hobby. At this point I was doing an evening course in business which would lead me onto the degree at Ulster university, which was also one evening a week while also working the other four and a half days a week. At the end of the two years of the HNC, I decided to take a years break from studying and work full time and decide what I wanted to do. But by the end of that summer I had seen the foundation degree on the NRC website and after a day or two of thinking about it, I realised that programming is what I really wanted to do, so I applied.

Foundation Degree

I really enjoyed my two years doing the foundation degree. I learned not only more python but also learned Java, C#, SQL, HTML and CSS and built an online booking system in Django. I also covered other topics such as networking, mathmatics for computing, relational databases and linux.

Goals & Aspirations

My overall goal at this time is to graduate from my degree with a first overall and to get a graduate role in software engineering as this is the area of computer science i most enjoy and where i have the most skills.
